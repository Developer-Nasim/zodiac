Terminal Specific IOT Platform (TIOT)

With real-time location information and assets status information TIOT platform will detect occurrence of key cargo handling events, safety rule violation, and other critical events, in digitized and non-intrusive way, and use them to automatically change job handling status, and generate next job handling order without any intervention of human being, while all events, real-time operation status will be vividly visualized in integrated monitoring window for human supervision.

Zodiac TIOT platform is specially designed IOT Platform for process automation and optimization in cargo handling operation. As a versatile integration platform, TIOT is designed to seamlessly connecting any type of assets used in cargo handling process, like cranes, horizontal cargo carriers, and other static, non-mobile assets.
